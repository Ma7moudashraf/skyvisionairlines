Cargo Operations

Sky Vision Airlines specializes in comprehensive air cargo transportation solutions designed to meet the unique requirements of diverse shippers and industries. Operating beyond regular scheduled services, our cargo charter flights offer unmatched flexibility, reliability, and customization for all types of freight operations. Air transport with Sky Vision Airlines ensures fast transit times, allowing businesses to minimize inventory holding costs and reduce lead times, enabling them to maintain optimal stock levels, improve supply chain performance, and achieve significant cost savings through reduced storage and quicker delivery cycles.

Cargo Operations

Our cargo operations are supported by advanced security protocols to guarantee the safe and reliable transportation of valuable and sensitive goods, with every shipment handled with precision, care, and full compliance with IATA regulations and international air transport standards. Sky Vision Airlines is equipped to manage a wide range of special cargo requiring unique handling procedures, including live animals (AVI) such as camels, bees, horses, goats, and fish; human remains(HUM) and dangerous goods (DG) in accordance with IATA standards; perishable goods(PER) like fruits, vegetables, meat, flowers, fish, and plant seeds; valuable cargo (VAL) including gold, diamonds, platinum, and other precious assets; as well as items with distinctive characteristics, such as strong-smelling goods like durian, perfumes, and essential oils, which are managed under strict handling protocols. With a steadfast commitment to safety, efficiency, and precision, Sky Vision Airlines continues to serve as a trusted partner for regional and international cargo clients, ensuring that every shipment reaches its destination securely, swiftly, and on time.
