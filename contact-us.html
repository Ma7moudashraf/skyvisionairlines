About Sky Vision Airlines

Connecting People. Powering Trade

Who We Are

Sky Vision Airlines, an emerging leader among Egyptian airlines, was established in 2022 with a clear vision to deliver exceptional passenger and cargo air services across Africa, Europe, and the Middle East.

The airline currently operates a modern fleet of six aircraft, comprising three passenger planes that ensure a comfortable, reliable, and safe travel experience, and three dedicated cargo aircraft that facilitate the efficient transport of goods through our advanced air freight services.

Our Role and Dedication

The company also plays an active role in supporting large national projects, offering innovative aviation solutions tailored to the diverse needs of our partners and clients.

Driven by a commitment to safety, performance, and customer satisfaction, Sky Vision Airlines continues to strengthen its presence in the regional aviation market, connecting people, businesses, and opportunities with precision and professionalism.

Our Mission

Sky Vision Airlines is committed to delivering safe, reliable, and innovative air transport solutions that connect people and strengthen trade across regions. Through a dedication to excellence, advanced technology, and customer-focused service, we aim to exceed expectations in every flight. Guided by our core values of safety, integrity, and progress, we strive to contribute to sustainable growth and create lasting value for our passengers, partners, and the communities we serve.

Our Vision & Commitment to Safety

To become the leading regional airline in Africa, Europe and the Middle East, recognized for excellence in passenger and cargo aviation, operational reliability, and innovative air transport solutions that connect people, markets, and opportunities with trust and precision.

Safety is our highest priority. Every flight is operated under the strict oversight of the Egyptian Civil Aviation Authority (ECAA) and in alignment with EASA and ICAO standards.

Our Safety Management System (SMS), Crew Training Programs, and Compliance Monitoring processes are continuously reviewed to meet international benchmarks.

We are actively progressing toward IOSA (IATA Operational Safety Audit) registration — a milestone that underscores our dedication to global aviation excellence.
